## 3 · Follow-up run after the meeting (21 Jul)

The *"complementary beyond static FC"* claim was tested in
[nb08](../../sandbox/jaime/08_activation_vs_reconfiguration.ipynb):

- Nested, same-fold: reconfiguration does **not** clearly add over single-condition (0-back)
  connectivity (ΔR² = +0.034, sd 0.023, under 2 sd).
- A simpler **task-activation contrast** (2-back − 0-back mean BOLD) predicts far better
  (r ≈ 0.60, cross-run 0.53), and connectivity adds nothing over it (ΔR² ≈ −0.003).
- The signal is trait-level activation (0-back activation alone ≈ 0.57), not connectivity-specific.

Sandbox result, for team discussion — not a decision. Waiting on Goutham's pipeline to see whether
his approach already accounts for it.
